CRITERIA FOR SOCKET



SOCKET is our most advanced project ever. SOCKET is a really powerful Food Delivery System which enables Foodish to Deliver our Excellent quality food to customers across Mars. However, due to us being a relatively new company and gaining sudden growth, it has not been possible for us to make enough SOCKET rockets and get them ready for delivery.
We have decided to give SOCKET delivery to customers who have placed at least 15 orders in the last month and who have been regular customers. This enables us to gain the trust of loyal customers who enjoy our food and also enables us to make SOCKET more widespread. We are trying our best to make SOCKET available to each and every customer within the next two years.
